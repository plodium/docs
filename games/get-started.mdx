<Note>
  Please keep in mind that your Billing Portal and Game Panel **are separate accounts**. While they share the same email address, they **do not share** a password by default.

  After your order is processed, look for an email titled "**Account Created**" You'll need to follow the link inside to **set a password** for your Game Panel. You can use the same password as your billing account if you'd like to keep things simple, but you must set it manually.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Game Panel">
    - Open [**panel.plodium.com**](https://panel.plodium.com/) in your browser.
    - Enter your account credentials (**email** and **password**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login">
    Double-check your **email/username** and **password**, then click **Log In** to proceed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  You've successfully logged into the Game Panel.
</Check>
