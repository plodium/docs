<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Billing Portal">
    - Open [**billing.plodium.com**](https://billing.plodium.com) in your browser.
    - Select the **Login** button located in the top navigation bar.
    - Enter your account credentials (**email** and **password**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Double-check your **email** and **password**, then click **Sign In** to proceed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Congratulations! You’ve successfully logged in to your billing account.
</Check>
